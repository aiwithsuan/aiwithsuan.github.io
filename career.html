My Professional Story

My career has been a continuous journey of learning, adapting and creating value through technology. Starting from software engineering, I gradually moved into enterprise systems, global manufacturing analytics, business intelligence, data warehousing and modern analytics engineering. Across three countries and multiple industries, my purpose has remained consistent:

Using technology and data to help organisations make better decisions.

Career Timeline

1999 - 2000

Beginning of Technology Journey

Started career in Malaysia as a software developer. Built foundation in:

  • Software development
  • Database programming
  • Application design
  • Problem solving

These early years shaped my engineering mindset and passion for building practical solutions.

2000 - 2005

Enterprise Systems & Consulting — Singapore

Worked across banking, ERP and logistics industries. Responsibilities included:

This period developed my ability to bridge technology and business needs.

2005 - 2017

A defining chapter of my career. Supported global manufacturing operations across Asia, Europe and North America.

  • Manufacturing analytics
  • Production KPI monitoring
  • Machine uptime analytics
  • Data availability monitoring
  • System modernisation
  • Global engineering collaboration

Key achievements:

  • Maintained more than 99% system availability
  • Reduced operational effort by more than 30%
  • Improved manufacturing visibility through data-driven solutions

This experience shaped my belief that data is not merely a technology asset — it is a catalyst for operational excellence. True transformation happens when trusted data, technology and people come together through strong collaboration, clear accountability and a common focus on business outcomes.
