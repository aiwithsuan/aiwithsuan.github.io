AI Knowledge Engineering Concepts

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Connecting AI with trusted data sources and organisational knowledge to provide context-aware insights.


Context-Augmented Generation (CAG)

Providing AI systems with business rules, domain knowledge and operational context to improve response quality.


Prompt Engineering

Designing effective human-AI interactions to help stakeholders ask better questions and receive more valuable insights.

Future Business Applications
